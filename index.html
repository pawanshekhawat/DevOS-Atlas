Help & Shortcuts

Canvas Navigation

  • Pan Workspace: Left-click and drag the background, or hold Space and drag, or middle-click and drag.
  • Zoom In / Out: Scroll mouse wheel, or click the + and - buttons.
  • New Document: Double-click on any empty area of the canvas.
  • Reset View: Click Reset on the header zoom panel.

Window Management

  • Move Window: Drag the window's header bar.
  • Resize Window: Drag the custom borders/handles on any side.
  • Layer Focus: Click anywhere on a window to bring it to the top.
  • Minimize: Click the minimize icon (➖) on the header to collapse content.
  • Edit Content: Click the edit icon (📝) in the window header to modify raw Markdown, then click it again to save/preview.
